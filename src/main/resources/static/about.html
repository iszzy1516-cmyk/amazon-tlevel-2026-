About T-Levels

T-Levels are a new 2-year technical qualification giving students the skills industry needs. They combine classroom learning with a meaningful industry placement.

Amazon partners with colleges across the UK to support the Digital Production, Design and Development T-Level, giving students real-world exposure to cloud computing, software engineering, and digital design.

This portal connects students, parents, and college staff with course information, application tools, and support resources.

Why T-Levels?

  • Designed with employers — including Amazon
  • 45-day industry placement
  • Equivalent to 3 A-Levels
  • Clear progression to university, apprenticeships, or employment
